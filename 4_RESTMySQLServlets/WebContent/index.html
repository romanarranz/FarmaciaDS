PharmacyS

Wellcome to our Pharmacy API REST!

U can access to all resources introducing the next urls on the list:

Getters Operations

  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/ctofservice
  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/ftocservice
  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/service/getPersonByIdXML/{id}
  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/service/getPersonByIdJSON/{id}
  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/service/getAllPersonInXML
  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/service/getAllPersonInJSON

Insert Operations

  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/service/savePerson/{fullname}/{age}

Update Operations

  • http://localhost:8080/JAXRS2MySQL/rest/service/savePerson/{id}/{fullname}/{age}

Additionally you can do a click on the previous links to select what uri you want test, when its done click on Test
